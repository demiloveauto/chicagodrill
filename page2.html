Key Artists

Chicago Drill has produced several influential rappers who shaped the genre and inspired others.

Chief Keef is often credited as the pioneer, while Lil Durk and King Von brought new styles and stories to the scene.

Chief Keef

Known for "I Don't Like" and starting the Drill movement.

Lil Durk

Blends melodic rap with Drill roots.

King Von

Famous for storytelling in his lyrics.

Drill's Influence

  1. Changed the sound of modern rap, making it raw and authentic.
  2. Inspired Drill scenes in cities like New York and London.
  3. Many artists use Drill beats and flows today.
