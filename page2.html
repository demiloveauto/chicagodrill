Chicago Drill Artists

Key Artists

Chicago Drill has produced several influential rappers who shaped the genre and inspired others.

Chief Keef is often credited as the pioneer, while Lil Durk and King Von brought new styles and stories to the scene.

  • Chief Keef: Known for "I Don't Like" and starting the Drill movement.
  • Lil Durk: Blends melodic rap with Drill roots.
  • King Von: Famous for storytelling in his lyrics.

Drill's Influence

  1. Drill music has changed the sound of modern rap, making it more raw and authentic.
  2. It inspired Drill scenes in other cities like New York and London.
  3. Many artists use Drill beats and flows in their music today.
