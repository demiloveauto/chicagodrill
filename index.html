History

Chicago Drill began in the early 2010s on the South Side of Chicago. It quickly became popular for its honest depiction of street life and its unique sound.

The genre was shaped by young artists who used music to share their experiences and struggles.

Cultural Impact

  • Drill music brought attention to Chicago's youth and their stories.
  • It influenced fashion, slang, and attitudes in hip-hop culture.
  • Many mainstream rappers have adopted Drill elements in their music.
  1. Drill is now popular in cities around the world.
  2. It continues to evolve and inspire new artists.
